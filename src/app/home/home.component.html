The Buckeye Mudder Club

Probably the toughest club on campus

"When is the last time you did something for the first time?" That is the premise of the Tough Mudder event series and The Buckeye Mudder Club here at The Ohio State University. A Tough Mudder is an 8-10 mile team-oriented obstacle course that challenges you physically, mentally, and emotionally. Whether you've never even heard of Tough Mudder or you are a seasoned Legionnaire, The Buckeye Mudder Club is the place to join fellow Buckeyes in a pursuit to enhance our collective athleticism, camaraderie, and teamwork.
