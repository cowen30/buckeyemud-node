Frequently Asked Questions


Is there a membership fee?
Yes. There is a non-refundable $40 fee for membership for the year. Membership includes the official Buckeye Mudder Club t-shirt, discounted entry to Tough Mudder Events, and access to group workouts with the club. Returning members from the previous year have a $30 non-refundable fee to retain membership status.
What will the workouts look like? What if I am not in shape?
There will be four workouts during the week. Strength and conditioning training will be two nights during the week and running workouts will take place twice a week as well. These workouts are optional but will be led by legionnaires Dana Furnas, Ryan Wuebben, and Evan Sanders who will be able to prepare you for everything the course can throw at you. These workouts are open to members of all fitness levels and leaders will ensure a safe, positive environment!
How will The Buckeye Mudder Club get to Tough Mudder events?
A month prior to the event, leaders will ask who is planning on participating at each given event. Packages will be available that will include Tough Mudder entry, transportation, and hotel accommodations (if necessary) at a discounted price. For some events at a further distance away, these packages will also include airfare. Members are welcome to make other accommodations but are encouraged to stay and travel with the group.
What different events is The Buckeye Mudder Club participating in?
The Buckeye Mudder Club will be participating in all offered Tough Mudder Events.

Tough Mudder Classic (8-10 miles): We will typically be participating in the flagship Tough Mudder course which is 8-10 miles and will push participants physically, mentally, and emotionally.

Tough Mudder 5k (3 miles): For Buckeye Mudders who are looking for a warm up or looking to get a feel for what Tough Mudder is about, we will participate in the Tough Mudder 5k, a 3 mile obstacle course great for people who haven't experienced Tough Mudder before.

Tougher Mudder (8-10 miles): For those looking for a competitive atmosphere, we will be participating in Tougher Mudder, the first start wave of the weekend which has podium awards for the top finishers.

Toughest Mudder (12 hours): For extreme Buckeye Mudders we will be participating in the Toughest Mudder event, a 12 hour challenge through the night pushing all the limits.

World's Toughest Mudder (24 hours): For the most insane Buckeye Mudders we will be participating in World's Toughest Mudder, a 24 hour challenge in the heart of the south, just outside Atlanta at the end of the year. This challenge is not for the feint of heart and will demand the most of Mudders both physically and mentally.
